Edutrip in Taiwan!

My name is Xenna from class 8B. I got the chance to explore and learn about Taiwan through the Edutrip program my school, Santa Laurensia held. There are 3 destinations you can go to: Hualien, Keelung, and Kaohsiung. My main destination was Kaohsiung, but we also got to visit Taipei, and a little bit of Taichung. With that, we were given a workbook with assignments given from different subjects. The assignments given are:

  • English: Creating a travel article
  • IPS: Interviewing
  • Bahasa Indonesia: Making a review (teks resensi) about a hotel
  • Mandarin: Interviewing our buddies and making a paragraph about them
  • Religion: Community service & Church
