═════ Religion - Community Service & Church ═════

For Religion, we were asked about the community service that we did along with what we learned about the Holy Family Catholic church.

Tzu Chi Foundation

In Tzu Chi Foundation, the community service we did was arranging plastic (removing the parts with texts or images) and arranging trash according to its type, such as paper, tin cans, glass, etc. Things with multiple components need to be broken down by machines. We also learned that all these trash can be recycled into many various items.

St Dominic High School

In St Dominican Highschool, we took accompanied the elderly with their activities. This school held a program for kids with special cases and the elderly. We learned sign language along with them, danced, and played some games.
